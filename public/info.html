2020 · Held in Place, Then Released

2020

Pandemic blockade.
Neurotech 3 → 18.

The world stopped. So I started writing — long-form rants on LinkedIn that nobody asked for and a few people actually read. While everyone froze, we built Neurotech from three to eighteen. Culture compounds faster than headcount when there's nowhere else to go.
